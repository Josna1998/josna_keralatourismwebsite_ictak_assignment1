Ernakulam

Ernakulam, the commercial capital of Kerala, is a renowned tourist destination too. It is a cosmopolitan city equipped with modern amenities, good communication facilities, excellent accommodation and well laid road, rail and air connectivity with rest of India. Flanked by the Arabian Sea, Ernakulam is embraced by outstanding natural beauty. The Dutch Palace, Chinese Fishing Nets and Jewish Synagogue are popular attractions here. There is so much to see and explore in Ernakulam, it has various historical sites, temples, forts, beaches, museums, and other tourist places to attract travelers from all around the world. No doubt that Ernakulam is an ultimate destination for a globetrotter, who aims to explore as much as they can in their single visit to 'God's Own Country.
